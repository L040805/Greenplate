Dynamic Pricing

AI-powered discount advice based on freshness, expiry, waste history and stock levels.

How the advice engine works
Each dish is scored using four factors: days until expiry of its worst-case ingredient, freshness rating (1-10), waste history (only counted when urgency already exists), and stock level. Scores determine the recommended discount. Dishes with all-fresh ingredients receive no recommendation.
